All about Grizzly bears

Characteristics

Grizzly bears are able to stand on their hind legs. Cubs stay with their mothers for a few years to learn from her and keep safe. Females have broader backs & backsides than males. They have very strong museles. Bears are quite intelligent. Bears have lovely brown and golden furr. Bears are quite violent to humanity though. Bears fatten up and get bigger during the hot summer. Bears are massive and bulky. Bears hibenate in the cold winter when they can sleep.

Diet

Bears shred their food before eating it because they can't eat it whole. Bears are very strong and can jump far into rivers to catch food. Bears eat fish for food to survive themselves and also others. Bears swim to catch fish in the river,they eat it with their paws and sharp teeth. Bears also eat insects like moths and they eat berries. They have rear teeth to crunch food and they have canine teeth to catch fish.

Habitat

You will normally find bears in America and Canada in forests/meadows. Sometimes they live in big forests near lakes/rivers where there are tasty fish for them to eat and keep alive. At first , bear cubs stay with their mothers for a few years before they are strong enough to go into the wild.
