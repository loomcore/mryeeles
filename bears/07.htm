Characteristics

Grizzly bears are brown with small eyes. When they are young they have a white ring around there neck. They may be cute but they are very dangerous and can run up to 35 miles per hour. Baby bears stay with there mum for two to three years. They have 42 teeth. Bears grow to about 5 to 6 feet.
