Facts about grizzly bears

Characteristics

Grizzly bears are one of the largest land carnivours in the 21st century. Young cubs have white collars and they stay with their mothers for a fue years. They can stand on their hind legs and run up to 40 Mph.They are solitary animals, They have claws up to 4 inches.They are an umbrella species.

Habitat

Grizzly bears live in dense forests near rivers. They also are found in meadows. They are asually found between Alaska to Mexico and California to Ohio. The male bears live far from their mother and the females live closer. There are about 1,800 left in the wild.

Diet

Grizzly bears are Omnivours but mostly eat meat like Elk, Deer and fish such as salmon. They shred their fish and sometimes eat fish that hasn't been finished . They catch there fish by diving in and sniffing them outYear old cubs wait for their mother to find their food. These bears also eat seeds, berries, roots, grass and fungi.
