Welcome

Welcome to Mr Yeeles' Web resource. This site is home to a blog detailing the experience of implementing the STEP4SEAs project in a Year 4 class, in a diverse city school in Cambridge, UK. It also hosts resources for classroom volunteers. Both are accessible from the sidebar.

If you are a member of our school community and would like to volunteer in the class, please complete the online registration form.

If you are not a member of the school community, but would still like to volunteer with us, please contact the school office.
